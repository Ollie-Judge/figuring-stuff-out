Ethics Our Story
We Pride ourselves on being able to provide you with the freshest, cleanest (free of toxins and chemicals) and also fully sustainable produce that is locally grown by our friends at farms and also local allotments dotted around London. as well as being huge supporters of quality produce, we ensure all our meat is free range, organic and free from toxins such as heavy metals and chlorine. In 1902 my great grandparents emigrated from italy taking with them only the bare essentials and a cook book from my great grandma's grandma, which contained all the recipe's they used when they first opened this restaurant. From there the business started to grow when people found out about our delicious food and we havent looked back since. we are still proudly family run, I am the manager (Giseppe Romano), my son Alberto is the chef, my wife Amalia and Daughter Alessandra are the waitresses.
